Stepping inside, the restaurant brings back memories of the vintage 1980s Hong Kong shop: colorful neon signs, contrasting vibrant colors and an open concept kitchen along with a tea bar. “Bao Bei” is nostalgic yet harbour the modern twist within its details.
Bao Bei’s capacity is up to 150 seats with flexibility in options of decoration for the best service of events and banquets.

Be prepared to experience the finest selection of Chinese cuisine, ranging from hand- made Dimsum to famous roasts such as the HongKong’s favourite roasted Goose.
At Bao Bei, we put the satisfaction and well-being of customers at the top priority. With impeccable customer service, our goal is to satisfy your culinary experience.
